Are you tired of losing your army lists? Want somewhere to share your perfectly tuned cheese machines?

By having a WarDex account, you can create your own personal profile, where you can store army lists for specific forces, pictures of the models in the armies, backstories for your generals and much more!

WarDex will provide many tools for users who wish to run or be part of a community. Form a clan, or a club and it will have everything you need. A few of the intended features are below:

Painting competitions

Who's got the sexiest models in your group?

Campaigns

Can you conquer the worlds you inhabit?

Structure

Structure your group, show 'em who's boss!

FAQ

What does this cost?
There will be no inherent cost to owning a WarDex account, I hope to have them completley free for everyone!.

Unfortunatley, no. This would be breaking many copyrights that certain companies hold, and they're not exactly leniant in the breaking of them.
